PRE-DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT — OLD-SCHOOL NICHES
(logistics, packaging, manufacturing, distribution, trucking, etc.)
================================================================
Use case: prospect replied to outreach, gave number or said "ok"
Goal: confirm relevance in 60 sec, close on 20-min Google Meet
Key rule: this call is NOT the discovery — it's just booking the discovery


--- LIVE CALL ---

Hey [NAME], Leonid here — from the emails about [OUTREACH_TOPIC].

[PAUSE — wait for "hey" / "yeah" / "uh-huh"]

Good. So — I work with [ICP_DESCRIPTION],
help them find the right [PARTNER_TYPE] when they need [SERVICE].

Quick question — is that actually something you're looking to grow right now,
[VALUE_PROP_QUESTION]?

[PAUSE — wait for yes/no]

[If yes:]

Got it. A few things I want to walk you through on a quick call:
- what kind of [ICP] I work with and what they're typically looking for
- [SPECIFIC_2 — volume / size / lane / budget / timing]
- how I vet [ICP] before I make any intro — so it's not a waste of your time

Easier to do properly than over the phone right now.
20 minutes max — [DAY_OPTION_1] or [DAY_OPTION_2], which works better?

[PAUSE — wait for a day]

Perfect. I'll send a Google Meet right after this.


--- VOICEMAIL ---

Hey [NAME], Leonid here — from the emails about [OUTREACH_TOPIC].

Wanted to quickly check if [VALUE_PROP_STATEMENT] is something you're actively growing right now.

I've got a few things I want to walk you through — easier on a quick call.
Try you again tomorrow, or feel free to call me back at [YOUR_NUMBER].


--- DAY OPTIONS LOGIC ---

Monday    → "tomorrow or Wednesday"
Tuesday   → "tomorrow or Thursday"
Wednesday → "tomorrow or Friday"
Thursday  → "tomorrow or Monday"
Friday    → "tomorrow or Monday"
Saturday  → "Monday or Tuesday"


--- PLACEHOLDERS GUIDE ---

[NAME]               - first name of prospect
[OUTREACH_TOPIC]     - e.g. "the Midwest freight" / "the insurance intros"
[ICP_DESCRIPTION]    - e.g. "mid-market manufacturers in Ohio and Michigan"
[PARTNER_TYPE]       - e.g. "carriers" / "restoration companies" / "vendors"
[SERVICE]            - e.g. "FTL capacity" / "contents restoration partners"
[VALUE_PROP_QUESTION] - e.g. "getting more FTL freight from manufacturers"
[SPECIFIC_2]         - most relevant second detail for this niche
[ICP]                - short label for the leads you bring (shippers, manufacturers, etc.)
[DAY_OPTION_1/2]     - use DAY OPTIONS LOGIC above based on current day
[YOUR_NUMBER]        - your callback number


--- RULES FOR THIS SCRIPT ---

1. Pause after intro — let him recognize you before you pitch
2. One yes/no question only — do not open freight/industry terminology
3. "Easier to do on a quick call" — honest reason to book, not an excuse
4. Never say "I have X shippers ready right now" unless you do — framing is "I work with / I monitor"
5. Name 3 specifics after "got it" — makes the meeting feel concrete and worth 20 min
6. Two day options, not open-ended "when works for you" — forces a decision
7. Voicemail: no questions, leave intrigue, announce you'll call back
