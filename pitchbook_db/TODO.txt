PITCHBOOK DATABASE — RESULTS & NEXT STEPS
==========================================

WHAT WAS DONE
-------------
1. Unpacked two PitchBook archives:
   - June 2025 (june_2025/) — 343 CSV files, contact-level data, pre-filtered REV $5M+
   - September 2025 (sep_2025/) — 33 XLSX files, company+contact data, mixed sectors

2. Analyzed structure:
   - June CSVs: obfuscated PitchBook column names (entity-hover, ellipsis etc.) — contacts
   - Sep XLSX: clean columns (Company Name, Email, Revenue etc.) — companies with contacts
   - Employee count column does NOT exist in any file — cannot filter by headcount

3. Combined all files into one CSV (combine.py):
   - Raw records:    2,226,296
   - After dedup:      909,208  → all_leads.csv
   - Skipped 2 files (corrupt/wrong format)

4. Filtered for System Hustle cold outreach targets:
   - Criteria: B2B sectors, email-first GTM, 1000+ potential clients, $10k+ ACV
   - Result:    273,560 leads → target_leads.csv
   - Top sectors: SaaS, Consulting B2B, Business Productivity Software,
     Fintech, IT Consulting, Financial Software, Marketing Tech, AI/ML

KEY FINDINGS
------------
- No employee count data in any source file (would need Apollo/Clearbit to enrich)
- Revenue column exists only in Sep XLSX files (not in June CSVs, those are pre-filtered $5M+)
- ~60% of raw records were duplicates across sector files (same person in SaaS + FinTech etc.)
- Best performing sectors by volume: SaaS (56k), TMT (90k), Manufacturing (101k), Consulting (33k)

OUTPUT FILES

target_leads.csv  — 273,560 leads, B2B outreach-ready sectors only
