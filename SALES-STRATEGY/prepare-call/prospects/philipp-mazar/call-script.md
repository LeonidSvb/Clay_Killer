## PITCH

"Most search firms grow through relationships and referrals. That works — until the brief's already public and other firms are pitching the same thing.

Since what you do is mostly partner and team placements — the signals that matter are different from regular hiring signals. Things like:
- A partner leaves a firm — the firm needs a replacement, and that partner (sometimes with their team) is now on the market
- A firm opens a new office or location — that office needs a partner to lead it
- A firm publicly says it's expanding a practice area — transfer pricing, M&A tax, whatever — that means they're about to bring in someone senior to build it
- Generational handover at a firm's leadership — an older partner retiring creates an opening

I track things like JUVE for partner moves, LinkedIn for 'started new position as Partner,' and firm press releases. When I see one of these fire, I confirm it's real, then connect you to the right person before it's a formal search.

You get qualified conversations with firms that have a live, real trigger. Not a list — the right account, right moment."

[PAUSE]
