## Kickoff Templates

Two files — use for every new client kickoff.

### kickoff-deck-template.html
Client-facing. Share screen during the call.
Replace all [PLACEHOLDERS] before the call.

Key placeholders:
- [CLIENT] — client name / company
- [DATE] — kickoff date
- [COUNTRIES] — primary target markets
- [EXPANSION REGION] — secondary markets
- [N] — company counts
- [SIGNAL TYPES] — signals relevant to their niche
- [SALARY] — minimum salary threshold

### kickoff-script-template.html
Internal only — never share screen on this one.
Your navigation guide during the call.
Replace [CLIENT NAME], [PRIMARY MARKETS], [EXPANSION MARKETS], [CAPACITY NOTE].

### Framework (same for every client)
1. Opening — share deck, set agenda
2. Walk through strategy — 2 channels
3. His current process + time availability
4. Candidate-as-hook (if applicable)
5. Market scope — expand not narrow
6. Qualified intro definition — get explicit confirmation
7. Workflow — comms channel, response SLA
8. Reality check — biggest concern
9. Next steps

### Qualified intro definition (universal)
Right company + right country + person who makes hiring decisions said yes to a conversation.
If client calls from signal card and conversation leads somewhere = counts.
