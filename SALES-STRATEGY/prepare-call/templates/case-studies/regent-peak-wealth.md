## Client
Regent Peak Wealth Advisors — independent advisory firm serving high-net-worth individuals: business owners, entrepreneurs, executives, multi-generational families, trustees, board directors.

## Challenge
Opening conversations with prospects who genuinely aligned with the firm's positioning. Broad low-fit prospecting undermines trust in wealth management — needed precision, not volume.

## What Was Done
Evaluated market through Regent Peak's existing ideal client profile.
Identified people whose circumstances matched the firm's advisory strengths.
Filtered for profile alignment, not just wealth level.
Structured outreach that felt appropriate for high-net-worth recipients — trust-based, not pitch-based.

## Results (45 days)
- 6 qualified introductions opened with ICP-matched prospects
- High-fit conversations only — people the firm was already equipped to serve

## When to use
Best for pitching to clients in high-ticket, relationship-driven B2B sales where trust matters more than volume. Good proof that the system works for long sales cycles, not just transactional deals.
