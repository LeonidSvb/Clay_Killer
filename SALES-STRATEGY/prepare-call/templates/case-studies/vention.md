## Client
Vention — manufacturing automation platform. Helps companies design, order, and assemble custom industrial equipment.

## Challenge
- Could not reach decision-makers in manufacturing sectors
- Sales team overloaded with high lead volume, inconsistent outcomes
- Low conversion rates, disorganized processes, poor targeting
- Heavy reliance on expensive PPC with high acquisition costs

## What Was Done
Deep research into prospect pain points: production inefficiencies, long lead times, quality inconsistencies.
Built automated personalized email outreach system targeting those specific challenges.
Intelligent lead segmentation, continuous messaging optimization, prioritized high-intent prospects to reduce sales team workload.

## Results (one quarter)
- +$85K in additional revenue directly from improved lead generation
- Streamlined prospecting — sales team focused only on qualified opportunities
- Stronger client engagement, increased repeat business
- Reduced dependency on paid advertising

## When to use
Best case study for pitching to companies in automation, industrial tech, warehouse tech, or any vendor with a complex B2B sales cycle looking to build predictable pipeline.
